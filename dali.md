# Beyond Fisher Forecasting for Cosmology

Advanced Statistical Methods for Cosmological Parameter Estimation.

## Overview

Ryan et al. (2023) — This research explores advanced statistical methods that go beyond traditional Fisher matrix forecasting techniques for cosmological parameter estimation, enabling more accurate predictions for upcoming surveys.

- **Published:** Physical Review D 107, 103506 (2023)
